# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kasa Defterim (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects user data.

## Application Purpose
Kasa Defterim is a finance tracking application designed to help users record income, expenses, suppliers, debts, and cash balances for personal or small business use.

## Information We Collect
Kasa Defterim does not require account creation.

The app collects only the data that users manually enter, such as:
- Income and expense records
- Supplier names
- Debt and payment information
- Notes related to financial transactions

No sensitive personal data is collected automatically.

## Data Usage
- All entered data is used solely to provide in-app financial tracking features.
- Data is not sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

## Data Storage
- All data is stored locally on the user’s device.
- If enabled by the user, data may be synced using Apple-provided services such as iCloud.
- We do not operate or maintain external servers.

## Third-Party Services
Kasa Defterim uses only Apple-provided services when necessary:
- Apple iCloud (optional, user-enabled)
- Apple In-App Purchases (for optional premium features)

No third-party analytics or advertising services are used.

## User Control
Users can:
- Edit or delete their data at any time within the app
- Remove all stored data by deleting the app
- Disable iCloud syncing via device settings

## Children’s Privacy
Kasa Defterim is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
